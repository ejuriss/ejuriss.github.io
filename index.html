Twinkle Toes

In 2020, I created a coming of age short film called Twinkle Toes. It follows the story of a young boy who has a passion for dance, however, he recieves rude notes from his classmates everyday about how he should stop 'being a girl' and become 'more masculine'. He loses his passion until be recieves an encouraging note and begins to love himself and his passion once again; breaking the boundries of sterotypes.

Imagination

In 2021, I created a coming of age/film-noir short called Imagination. On Tommy's birthday he got the toy he always wanted, however, when he left it just for a moment he found it 'murdered' (cut up). Tommy creates this noir reality in his mind, and become a detective: who killed it? what was the motive? Concluding that the murderer only did it out of jealously for Tommy's childhood and few responsiblities he had while they suffered.
